# PostVelocity Quick Start Tutorial ⚡

## **From Zero to Publishing in 10 Minutes**

This lightning-fast tutorial gets you up and running with PostVelocity in just 10 minutes. Perfect for new users who want immediate results.

---

## **⏱️ 10-Minute Setup Checklist**

### **Minutes 1-2: Account Setup & Plan Selection**
✅ **Choose Your Plan:**
- **Free Trial**: 7 days, 50 content generations
- **Starter Plan**: $29/month, 100 posts, 3 companies
- **Professional Plan**: $79/month, 500 posts, 10 companies

✅ **Create Account:**
1. Enter email address
2. Create secure password
3. Verify email
4. Choose plan or start trial

### **Minutes 3-4: Create Your First Company**
✅ **Company Setup:**
1. Click **"+ Add Company"**
2. Enter company name
3. Select industry (Construction, Environmental, Safety, etc.)
4. Add website URL
5. Describe target audience
6. Set brand voice (Professional, Friendly, Technical)
7. Click **"Create Company"**

**Pro Tip**: Be specific with your industry selection - this helps AI generate better content!

### **Minutes 5-6: Connect Social Media Platforms**
✅ **OAuth Integration:**
1. Go to **Content Hub** tab
2. Find **Social Media Connections** section
3. Click **"Connect"** next to your primary platforms:
   - **Instagram** (for visual content)
   - **LinkedIn** (for B2B reach)  
   - **Facebook** (for community building)
4. Complete OAuth authorization for each platform
5. Verify green connection indicators appear

**Pro Tip**: Start with your 3 most important platforms. You can add more later!

### **Minutes 7-8: Upload Key Media Files**
✅ **Media Upload:**
1. Go to **Media Library** tab
2. Select your company from dropdown
3. Drag & drop 5-10 photos:
   - Team photos
   - Workplace/office images
   - Products/equipment
   - Your logo
4. Add descriptions and tags
5. Choose appropriate categories

**Pro Tip**: AI uses these images to enhance your content - quality matters!

### **Minutes 9-10: Generate & Publish First Content**
✅ **Content Creation & Publishing:**
1. Return to **Content Hub** tab
2. Click **"Smart Generate"**
3. Watch AI select trending topic
4. Review generated content for each platform
5. Select connected platforms
6. Click **"🚀 Publish to Connected Platforms"**
7. Confirm successful publishing notifications

**🎉 Congratulations! You just published AI-generated content to multiple platforms in minutes!**

## **🆘 Quick Troubleshooting**

### **Common Issues & Instant Fixes**

**❓ "My platform won't connect"**
✅ **Solution**: Use business/creator account, ensure admin permissions

**❓ "Content isn't posting"**
✅ **Solution**: Check OAuth connections, verify account status

**❓ "AI content doesn't match my brand"**
✅ **Solution**: Update company description and brand voice settings

**❓ "Low engagement rates"**
✅ **Solution**: Post at optimal times, use trending hashtags, add media

**❓ "Running out of trial generations"**
✅ **Solution**: Focus on high-impact content, consider plan upgrade
