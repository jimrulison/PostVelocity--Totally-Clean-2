### **[00:30 - 01:45] Key Metrics Explained**
```
PostVelocity tracks dozens of metrics, but these 5 are crucial:

1. Engagement Rate - Percentage of people who interact with your content
2. Reach - Total unique people who saw your content
3. Impressions - Total number of times your content was displayed
4. Click-Through Rate - Percentage who clicked your links
5. ROI - Return on investment from social media efforts

Think of it like a funnel:
- Reach gets people to see your content
- Engagement gets them interested
- Clicks get them to your website
- Conversions get them to buy

Each metric tells part of the story.
```

### **[01:45 - 02:30] Engagement Rate Deep Dive**
```
Engagement Rate is your most important metric. Here's why:

Engagement Rate = (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Total Reach × 100

PostVelocity's benchmarks:
- Instagram: 1-3% = Good, 3-6% = Excellent
- Facebook: 0.5-1% = Good, 1%+ = Excellent  
- LinkedIn: 2-5% = Good, 5%+ = Excellent
- TikTok: 5-15% = Good, 15%+ = Excellent

High engagement means:
- Your content resonates with your audience
- Social media algorithms promote your content more
- You build authentic relationships with customers

Low engagement means it's time to change your strategy.
```

### **[02:30 - 03:15] ROI Calculation & Interpretation**
```
ROI shows the business impact of your social media efforts.

Formula: ROI = (Revenue Generated - Cost of Social Media) / Cost × 100

Example:
- Revenue from social media: $10,000
- Cost of social media: $1,000
- ROI = ($10,000 - $1,000) / $1,000 × 100 = 900%

PostVelocity automatically tracks:
- Revenue attributed to social media
- Cost per lead
- Conversion rates by platform
- Customer lifetime value

ROI Benchmarks:
- 200-500% = Good return
- 500-1000% = Excellent return
- 1000%+ = Outstanding return

If ROI is below 200%, it's time to optimize.
```
